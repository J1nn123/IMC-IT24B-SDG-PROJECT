Our Company

Our company (IMC) is dedicated to ensuring seamless integration of all students to any school, regardless of background, in the hopes of inclusively providing fair opportunities for all.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to enable students sufficient information on our school's resources and services, particularly, PCs and Printers, in order to better their learning experience. We also aim to help freshmen familiarize with the two aforementioned, so they can fully utilize them.

Our Vision

Complete and efficient utilization of the school's services and resources using the internet to bridge space and time to fulfill the needs of the students.

Our Values

    Innovation: Continuously exploring new ways to solve problems and deliver value.

    Integrity: Acting with honesty and transparency in all that we do.

    Collaboration: Working together to achieve greater success.

    Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in every project and service.
